## 5-MINUTE PITCH (stage version)

### [0:00–0:30] THE HOOK

> "Quick question. Raise your hand if you can name every elected official who governs you. All of them — local, regional, national.
>
> Nobody? That's the problem.
>
> You can't build a parallel society until you can see what you're paralleling."

### [0:30–1:30] THE GOVERNANCE AGENT

> "We built a Governance Agent — a personal AI political advisor for every human on Earth. It discovers who governs you, what laws apply to you, and alerts you when things change — before they affect your life, not after.
>
> One human, one agent. Verified by World ID. No government permission required.
>
> Your agent runs continuously. It watches elections, legislation, regulatory changes. It tells you: these 33 people make decisions that affect your daily life. Here are the 12,847 laws you're subject to. Here's what's changing next month.
>
> That's layer one: awareness. You can't opt out of a system you can't see."

### [1:30–3:30] THE ECONOMY — TIME PROTOCOL

> "Layer two is the economy. And this is where it gets interesting.
>
> TIME Protocol is a parallel currency where **getting paid is what creates the money**.
>
> Here's how it works:
>
> When someone pays you for your work — an hour of coding, an hour of teaching, an hour of building — that payment transaction mints two things:
>
> **One:** New TIME tokens. ERC-20 on Ethereum and NEAR. One TIME per hour. Real, tradeable digital currency.
>
> **Two:** A Work NFT. A soulbound, non-transferable record of that work — what you did, when, for whom, what skills you used. This is your portable work history. It belongs to you forever. No employer can take it. No platform can delete it.
>
> So the act of commerce itself — paying a human for their time — is the mint event. No mining. No central bank. No venture capital printing tokens. Just humans paying humans, and the protocol recording it.
>
> **[pause]**
>
> Think about what that means.
>
> In the current system, money is created by banks issuing debt, or by central banks printing it. The people who do the actual work never create the money. They just receive it — after banks and institutions take their cut.
>
> In TIME Protocol, workers create the money. The payment IS the creation. There's no middleman. There's no money printer. Every TIME token in existence was created because a real human did real work and another human paid them for it.
>
> Maximum: 24 TIME per day per human. One birthright TIME just for being alive — that's your UBI, built into the protocol — plus up to 23 earned through verified work. No exceptions. No whales."

### [3:30–4:30] THE PARALLEL SYSTEM

> "Now here's the strategy for parallel society.
>
> You don't replace a system by protesting it. You replace it by building something better and letting people migrate.
>
> **Step one: Replicate.** Your Governance Agent maps the entire existing system. Every official. Every law. Every regulation. You see exactly what governs you today.
>
> **Step two: Commerce.** Start working in TIME. Get paid — the payment mints new TIME and records your work. Trade that TIME for Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins, or spend it directly. Real commerce, day one.
>
> **Step three: Allocate locally.** Instead of 'investing' — which means giving money to strangers — you allocate TIME to things you know. The café down the street. The repair shop. The community garden. Your Governance Agent surfaces these local opportunities.
>
> **Step four: Govern.** Stake your TIME, gain proportional voice. Not just in the parallel system — your agent helps you allocate voice in the traditional system too. Use both. Shift as the parallel proves itself.
>
> This is infrastructure for a gradual, voluntary transition. No revolution required. Just better infrastructure."

### [4:30–5:00] THE ASK

> "TIME Protocol is the economic foundation that the parallel society movement has been missing.
>
> You have the philosophy. You have the community. You have the will.
>
> What you need is infrastructure: identity, governance awareness, and a real economy where paying people for their work is what creates the money.
>
> That's what we built. It's launching in 2026.
>
> We're looking for pilot communities. If your community wants to be among the first to run a parallel economy on TIME Protocol, talk to me after this.
>
> **democracy.earth**"

## Q&A TALKING POINTS

**"How is TIME different from other crypto?"**

> Most crypto is mined by machines or pre-minted by founders. TIME is minted exclusively by the act of paying a human for their work. No payment, no new TIME. The economy grows exactly as fast as humans do actual work — not faster, not slower.

**"What prevents fake work verification?"**

> Both parties sign the transaction. The payer and the worker. If I claim I worked 8 hours for you and you didn't agree, the mint doesn't happen. Long-term, reputation and work history on-chain create accountability. Your Work NFT history IS your reputation.

**"Can I actually buy things with TIME?"**

> Yes. TIME trades freely on decentralized exchanges — swap it for ETH, BTC, stablecoins, or spend it directly with anyone who accepts it. The more people in the parallel economy, the more you can do directly in TIME without converting.

**"What about the birthright TIME — the 1 per day?"**

> That's the UBI layer. Every verified human gets 1 TIME per day they've been alive. It's minted when you claim it via World ID. This ensures everyone has a baseline — a dignity floor — even if they can't work. But the main engine of the economy is earned TIME: payment for work.

**"How does Waku / Logos fit in?"**

> Waku is the communication layer. It's how Governance Agents share intelligence, how TIME offers get posted, how communities coordinate — all without central servers. No one can shut it down or surveil it. If you're building parallel society, you need parallel communications. Waku is that.

**"How does this help parallel society specifically?"**

> Three things you need for a functioning parallel society: identity that doesn't depend on governments, governance awareness so you know what you're opting out of, and a real economy. TIME Protocol provides all three. The Governance Agent maps the existing system. TIME gives you an alternative economy. Waku gives you censorship-resistant coordination. Together, it's a complete operating system for parallel society.

**"When can I use it?"**

> We're onboarding pilot communities in 2026. If your community wants to be first, let's talk today.

## WHAT NOT TO SAY

- ~~"The Great Reset"~~ — Save for internal/strategic contexts. The Parallel Society audience wants action now, not a date to wait for.
- ~~"UBI"~~ — Lead with "payment creates money." Birthright TIME is a feature, not the headline.
- ~~"Blockchain"~~ — Say "protocol" or "on-chain." The audience knows what blockchain is; the word adds nothing.
- ~~"Decentralized"~~ — Say "no one controls it" or "can't be shut down." More concrete.
- ~~"Tokenomics"~~ — Say "how the money works."
